CHUAN

It is the most distinctive cuisine in China and the largest folk cuisine. Originated in Sichuan and Chongqing, it features hemp and spicy. The appearance of Sichuan cuisine can be traced back to the Qin and Han Dynasties. In the Song Dynasty, the genre was formed. After the pepper was introduced into China in the late Ming and early Qing dynasties.

Sue

Ingredients are rigorous, they pay attention to color matching, modeling and separating the four seasons. Cooking skills are well-known as having stew, braise and simmer; Pay attention to mixing soup, keep the original juice, make the taste peaceful. Use vegetables in. One Huaiyang dishes, select the materials and do knife works fastidiously, good at making soup.

Lu

The taste is salty and fresh. It pays attention to the quality of raw materials, fresh up by a use of salt, grow the freshness with soup, seasoning emphasis on salty fresh pure, highlights the flavor. The chefs use salt and fresh master cooking, good at making soup and seafood.

Yue

Mainly fresh and fragrant. Select ingredients subtly, fresh and not common, tender and not raw, oily and not greasy. Good at stir-fry, the chefs need to master the right temperature and oil. Also compatible with many western dishes, it pays attention to the momentum and class of food.
